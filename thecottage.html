The Cottage

More than just a building.

A House Built On Hope.

Community built by extending compassion as we seek to understand our guest hopes and fears. Integrity in offering our best as we seek to be good stewards of the donations we have been entrusted with by exhibiting transparency and accountability. Excellence by offering our best to our guest.

Mary Beth's Cottage has grown as an idea over the past years, and has plans to be developed into a beautiful space able to handle the various needs of the patients who will find their home here. With multiple floors, patients needs can be met quickly and efficiently with a staff happy to serve.
